Hi there! My name is Joshe and welcome to my learning journal.

Dream while you're awake because when you're sleeping, you don't get to choose your dreams. That quote is from a Spanish rapper named AMBKOR and it still resonates in every corner of my soul and mind. I started studying coding with Udemy, but I soon realized I needed feedback from experts and the opportunity to connect with a community of like-minded peers. Therefore, I decided to enroll as a bootcamp student in the Frontend Developer Career Path. This journal captures some of the experiences I've had on this incredible learning journey.

How I stay committed to learning

It's all about the big goal. I want to become a professional developer, and that's my goal. It's said that discipline defeats motivation, so no matter how busy I am, it's crucial to do something every day. Another significant aspect is accountability, and that's one of the reasons I enrolled in Scrimba's bootcamp.

How I got Started

Once upon a time, in the realm of my workplace, a weariness began to settle within me. I grew tired of the repetitive tasks, the manual labor involved in managing inventories and analyzing data. It was a monotonous routine that left me yearning for something more. In my quest for a solution, I turned to the reliable aid of Excel spreadsheets. They offered a glimmer of hope, allowing me to semi-automate the cumbersome process of data input. As I delved into this realm of organized cells and formulas, a flicker of inspiration reignited within me—a spark I had thought had long faded away. It was then that I rediscovered my true passion—the joy of building and problem-solving. This newfound revelation became the catalyst that propelled me forward. With renewed determination, I made a resolute decision—to invest my energy and embrace the path of becoming a professional web developer. And so, the next chapter of my story began, fueled by the flames of passion and a deep desire to forge a new future in the world of web development.
